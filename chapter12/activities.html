Activities at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort has 5 miles of hiking trails and is adjucent to a state park. Go it alone or join one of the our guided hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean Kayaks are available for guest use.

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for bird watching at Pacific Trails, we offer guided bird watching at sunrise several times a week.
